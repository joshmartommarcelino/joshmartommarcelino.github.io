GAME MECHANICS

Master the intricate fighting systems of Guilty Gear Strive. From fundamental techniques to advanced strategies, these mechanics form the backbone of high-level play.

Wall Break

Break the stage wall by cornering your opponent and landing attacks. This causes a cinematic transition and extra damage.

It also gives your character temporary buffs like more attack power.

Roman Cancel

Slows down the opponent or cancels your move, letting you continue your combo or confuse your enemy.

Useful for both attack and defense strategies.

Burst

A special escape move that uses part of your Burst gauge. It can stop combos and push your enemy away.

Use it wisely to get out of tough situations.

Tension Gauge

This meter fills up as you move and fight. Use it for powerful moves like Roman Cancels and Overdrives.

Managing it well is key to winning tough matches.

Overdrive

Super moves that take half your Tension Gauge. Each character has their own unique Overdrive.

These attacks are flashy and powerful, perfect for finishing off an opponent.

Faultless Defense

Hold two defense buttons to activate. This pushes the opponent away and blocks chip damage.

It costs Tension but can help you stay safe during pressure.

Guilty Gear Strive - The most accessible yet technically deep fighting game in the series.
