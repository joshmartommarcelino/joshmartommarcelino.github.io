GAME MECHANICS

Master the intricate fighting systems of Guilty Gear Strive. From fundamental techniques to advanced strategies, these mechanics form the backbone of high-level play.

Wall Break

Once you've got the opponent cornered, landing more attacks will break the stage wall, sending the opponent flying to the next area in a thrilling cinematic.

In addition to dealing extra damage, Wall Break grants your character temporary buffs, such as added attack power.

Roman Cancel

Roman Cancel sends out a shockwave that slows down time for the opponent on hit. Use it while on offense to overwhelm your opponent's defenses.

You can also use it to cancel and extend your combos, opening up further depth to the game's combat system.

Burst

Burst is your emergency escape option, activated at the cost of half of the Burst gauge. It can break you free from combos and create distance.

When used strategically, Burst can completely turn the tide of battle and give you a second chance when cornered.

Tension Gauge

The Tension Gauge fills as you move forward, attack, or defend against opponent's moves. Use this meter to perform Overdrives, Roman Cancels, and other powerful techniques.

Managing your Tension Gauge efficiently is key to victory in high-level play.

Overdrive

Overdrives are character-specific super moves that consume 50% of your Tension Gauge. These flashy, powerful attacks can deal massive damage.

Each character has unique Overdrives that showcase their personality and fighting style, serving as both tactical options and spectacular finishers.

Faultless Defense

By holding two defense buttons while blocking, you can activate Faultless Defense which creates more pushback and prevents chip damage.

This defensive technique consumes Tension but can be essential for surviving pressure and creating space.
