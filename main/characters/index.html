Character Overview

Sol Badguy is the main protagonist of the Guilty Gear series and one of the most iconic characters in fighting games. As the original Guilty Gear, he has tremendous strength and resilience that makes him a formidable opponent.

With his aggressive playstyle and powerful moves, Sol is perfect for players who prefer an offensive approach. His simple yet effective moveset makes him an excellent choice for beginners while still offering depth for veteran players.

Key Moves

Gun Flame

236 + P

Sol fires a stream of flames along the ground. Great for spacing and creating pressure.

Volcanic Viper

623 + S/H

Sol's iconic dragon punch. Great anti-air move that can also be used as a combo ender.

Bandit Revolver

236 + K

A powerful spinning kick that can close distance quickly. Can be followed up with additional kicks.

Wild Throw

623 + D

A command grab that can catch opponents off guard. Good for mixing up your offense.

Strategy

Sol excels at close to mid-range combat with his powerful normals and special moves. His high damage output allows him to quickly turn the tide of battle with just a few successful hits. While Sol's ease of use makes him accessible to newcomers, mastering his Roman Cancel combos and knowing when to use his more situational moves will help you maximize his potential.

Key tactics include:

  • Use Gun Flame to control space and force approaches
  • Punish jump-ins with Volcanic Viper
  • Mix up your offense with Bandit Revolver and Wild Throw
  • Learn when to use Roman Cancels to extend combos and create pressure
